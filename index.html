About Us

Dansure Engineering Group Limited is a multi-disciplinary engineering and construction services company based in Accra, Ghana. We deliver high-quality, cost-effective, and sustainable solutions across mechanical (HVAC), electrical, plumbing, fire safety, security, ICT infrastructure, and building materials.

We aim to be Ghana’s most trusted name in engineering and construction, delivering excellence through every residential, commercial, and industrial project.

At Dansure, we don’t just build systems—we build lasting partnerships for sure.

Our Services

Fire & Security Systems

Fire alarms, sprinkler, fire hose, hydrants, fire extinguishers, suppression, CCTV, access control, and security audits.

Electrical Engineering

Smart lighting, backup power, energy audits, and reliable electrical systems.

Plumbing Systems

Installations, repairs, water filtration, and routine maintenance.

ICT Infrastructure

Structured cabling, Wi-Fi, server rooms, and cybersecurity solutions.

Mechanical (HVAC)

Design and maintenance of HVAC systems for efficient climate control.

Construction Materials

Project Management

Consultancy, coordination, site supervision, and post-project evaluations, testing and commision.
